## ◈ About

I am a **Senior DevOps & Multi-Cloud Architect** with over 15 years of production engineering experience across AWS, Azure, and GCP. My work spans cloud-native infrastructure design, enterprise DevOps transformation, and building engineers who operate confidently at scale.

I have personally trained **24,400+ engineers** — from first-timers to senior developers — and placed them at Amazon, Microsoft, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and product-based startups across India and internationally. Every concept I teach is drawn from real systems I have built, operated, and optimised.

I currently run **[Veera Cloud](https://veeracloud.shop/)**, a live cloud practice lab where learners get hands-on access to real infrastructure environments.
